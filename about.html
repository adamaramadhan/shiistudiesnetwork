About the Shii Studies Network

Our Mission

The Shii Studies Network serves as a vital academic platform connecting graduate students and emerging scholars who are dedicated to the study of Shiism across various disciplines. Our network fosters intellectual exchange, collaboration, and scholarly development within the field of Shii studies.

We aim to create a supportive environment where researchers can share their work, engage in meaningful discussions, and build lasting professional relationships. Through our events, publications, and collaborative initiatives, we strive to advance the understanding of Shii thought, history, culture, and contemporary issues.

The network welcomes scholars from diverse academic backgrounds including Islamic studies, history, anthropology, political science, literature, and related fields. We believe that interdisciplinary approaches enrich our understanding of Shii traditions and their contemporary relevance.

Our Goals

Our primary objectives include facilitating academic networking among graduate students and early-career scholars, organizing regular seminars and workshops featuring cutting-edge research, promoting interdisciplinary dialogue and collaboration, and supporting the professional development of our members through mentorship and research opportunities.

We also seek to bridge the gap between traditional Islamic scholarship and contemporary academic research, encouraging innovative methodologies and fresh perspectives in Shii studies. Through our initiatives, we hope to contribute to a more nuanced and comprehensive understanding of Shii communities worldwide.
