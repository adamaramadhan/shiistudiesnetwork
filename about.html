About the Shii Studies Network

The Shii Studies Network is a collaborative academic platform designed to connect graduate students, early-career researchers, and established scholars working in the diverse field of Shii studies. Our network spans multiple disciplines including Islamic studies, history, theology, sociology, anthropology, and political science.

Our mission is to foster intellectual exchange, facilitate collaborative research opportunities, and create a supportive community for scholars at all stages of their academic careers. We believe that the study of Shii Islam—encompassing its rich theological traditions, historical developments, contemporary manifestations, and global presence—benefits from interdisciplinary dialogue and cross-cultural perspectives.

Through regular seminars, workshops, conferences, and digital platforms, we aim to advance scholarly understanding of Shii communities, traditions, and contributions to Islamic civilization. We are committed to promoting rigorous academic research while maintaining respect for the lived experiences and perspectives of Shii communities worldwide.

The network welcomes scholars working on all aspects of Shii studies, including but not limited to: historical analysis, theological inquiry, contemporary social issues, political movements, cultural expressions, and transnational connections. We encourage both established researchers and emerging scholars to join our community and contribute to the ongoing scholarly conversation.
